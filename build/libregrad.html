LibreGrad | Information

LibreGrad the operating system is made on top of the Linux Kernel, or rather, a fork of the Linux Kernel. The Distro is made from zero.

We are currently working on our own init system, and rootfs. We will explain what that all means shortly. Currently, our main priority is documenting the official Linux Kernel, so we can study it, and know the internals for the full 100%.

At this point, we are in very earily stages of documenting and development. Yet we are making proper progress, Everything will be put together inside this documentation and repository. We are working on the basics of the kernel all the way to the working of each and every component.
